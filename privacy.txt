MCCDSigner PWA v0.2a privacy design

- The selected PDF is read and rendered in the browser.
- Pattern T OCR runs locally using bundled Tesseract.js and English language data.
- The app does not upload the PDF, signature or recognised text.
- The app contains no analytics or telemetry.
- A signed TEST PDF is created only after the user presses Generate review PDF.
- Reject discards the generated bytes from the page session.
- This prototype does not persist the selected PDF or signature in browser storage.
