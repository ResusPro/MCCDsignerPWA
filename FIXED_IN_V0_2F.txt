MCCDSigner PWA v0.2f — TEST ONLY

Root cause fixed:
- v0.2e could recognise the ME heading but its confirmation stage still depended on Tesseract's nested block/line structure for the lower labels.
- Browser OCR can merge or omit those line objects even when the words themselves are recognised.

v0.2f changes:
- requests Tesseract TSV output and parses individual word rows directly;
- recognises compound OCR tokens such as FULLNAME and GMCNUMBER;
- confirms the heading with lower identity/declaration and Signature/Date evidence in the correct vertical order;
- discards a dotted Signature/Date rule if it is mistaken for the outer lower border;
- shows the OCR evidence found in the warning if confirmation still fails;
- retains manual red-box adjustment and TEST-only output safeguards.
