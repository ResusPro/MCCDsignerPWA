MCCDSigner PWA v0.3

Detector redesign:
- Mirrors the proven Windows Pattern T sequence.
- OCR first confirms the unique heading "For the medical examiner to complete".
- Image geometry then identifies the enclosing top and bottom borders.
- Internal labels such as Full name, Declaration, Signature and Date are no longer mandatory for a clear rectangular box.
- If the enclosing rectangle is not confirmed, the result remains manual/fail-safe.
- Failure messages now include detector diagnostics.

All documents and OCR remain local to the browser.
This remains a TEST-ONLY prototype.
