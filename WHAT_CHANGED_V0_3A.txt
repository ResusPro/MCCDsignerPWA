MCCDSigner PWA v0.3a

This replaces the browser box-border scanner rather than changing another OCR threshold.

Cause of the repeated v0.2/v0.3 failures:
- the old scanner inspected a vertical band about 14 pixels deep;
- that merged printed text and dotted writing lines into false horizontal rules;
- OCR found the correct ME heading, but the rectangle was then rejected.

v0.3a:
- scans thin 5-pixel row bands;
- permits only tiny gaps;
- requires near-continuous long solid rules;
- groups the several pixels of each printed border into one rule;
- confirms aligned top and bottom borders around the OCR heading;
- shows the actual geometry diagnostic in the visible warning if it still fails.

The sample detector was independently checked against the synthetic form and three substantially different real-layout examples.
