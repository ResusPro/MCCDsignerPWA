MCCDSigner PWA v0.2g

ROOT CAUSE FIXED
Tesseract.js v7 returns headerless TSV rows in the browser. Earlier versions
assumed the first TSV row contained column names, so zero word records were
parsed even though the OCR heading itself was recognised.

V0.2g supports both headerless and headered TSV and therefore supplies the
recognised Full name, Declaration, Signature and Date words to Pattern T.

Also tightened fuzzy word matching so a single letter cannot be accepted as a
long label such as Qualifications.
