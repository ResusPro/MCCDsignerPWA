MCCDSigner PWA v0.2c detector patch

v0.2b correctly found the Medical Examiner heading but could still label the result as unconfirmed when Tesseract's Signature/Date line bounding box overlapped the outer lower rule.

v0.2c:
- keeps the heading OCR requirement;
- still uses the outer borders when they can be measured confidently;
- no longer starts its lower-border search strictly below the OCR line's y1 value;
- adds a safe text-structure confirmation fallback;
- the fallback requires the ME heading, the Signature/Date footer, and at least one of Full name / Qualifications / GMC number / Declaration;
- does not accept a heading-only estimate as automatic confirmation;
- retains manual adjustment and TEST-only output safeguards.
